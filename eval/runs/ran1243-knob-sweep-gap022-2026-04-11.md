**`pairwise_min_sources`:** Implementation keeps **only** midpoint cuts where primary and auxiliary agree within **ε/2**. It **does not** retain primary-only boundaries. With extras that never pair (runs **07**, **10`), fusion returns **no interior cuts** → **do not use** this policy for production merge unless the design is revised or extras are guaranteed to pair.

**`auxiliary_near_primary`:** Drops extras not within **ε** of a primary cut; with FN-oracle or noise lists, result **collapses to baseline** (runs **06**, **09**) when no extra lands near a primary peak.
